# Phishing Awareness Training – Online Module

## Internship Task | CodeAlpha

## Overview
This project is an interactive **Phishing Awareness Training online module** developed as part of my cybersecurity internship at **CodeAlpha**.  
The module is designed to educate users about phishing attacks, social engineering techniques, and best practices to avoid becoming victims of cyber fraud.

---

## Objectives
- To create awareness about phishing attacks and their impact
- To help users recognize phishing emails and fake websites
- To explain common social engineering techniques used by attackers
- To promote safe online practices
- To engage users through an interactive quiz

---

## Key Features
- Clear explanation of phishing and its types
- Identification of phishing emails and fake websites
- Explanation of social engineering tactics
- Best practices for online safety
- Interactive quiz for user engagement
- Simple and user-friendly interface

---

## Technologies Used
- **HTML** – Structure of the online module  
- **CSS** – Styling and layout  
- **JavaScript** – Quiz logic and interactivity
